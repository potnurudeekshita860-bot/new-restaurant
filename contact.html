FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you cater to dietary requirements? ▾
Yes! We have clearly marked vegan (V), gluten-free (GF), and nut-free options across our menu. Please inform us of any allergies at the time of booking or ordering.
What areas do you deliver to? ▾
We currently deliver across Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Madhapur, HITEC City, Kondapur, Gachibowli, Begumpet, and Secunderabad. Check out our delivery zones page for exact coverage.
Can I book EMBER for a private event? ▾
Absolutely. We offer full and partial restaurant buyouts for corporate events, birthday dinners, and intimate celebrations. Email events@emberdining.in with your requirements.
How far in advance should I book? ▾
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially for weekends. Same-day bookings are occasionally possible — call us directly to check availability.
What is your cancellation policy? ▾
Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before the booking time. Late cancellations or no-shows for groups of 6+ may incur a small fee.
Is there a dress code? ▾
We ask for smart-casual attire. We want you to be comfortable while still honouring the dining experience. No beachwear or sportswear please.
