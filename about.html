Our Story

Born From Fire,
Built on Passion

EMBER began in 2012 as a 12-seat supper club in a converted warehouse in Banjara Hills. Chef Rohan Malhotra had one vision: bring the honesty of open-fire cooking to Hyderabad — no pretence, no tricks, just incredible ingredients and real heat.

Twelve years later, we're a full-scale restaurant and delivery operation, but we've never lost that original fire. Every dish still passes through the same open-flame kitchen, and every recipe is Rohan's own.

The Beginning

A Supper Club That Became a Movement

It started with 12 seats, a secondhand grill, and an open invitation posted on social media. Within three months, the waiting list was 200 people long. What Chef Rohan had stumbled onto wasn't just a restaurant concept — it was a community around real food.

The philosophy was simple: use the best seasonal ingredients, cook them over live fire, and let the flavours speak for themselves. No foam, no gels, no theatre — just food that tastes extraordinary.

🌿
Our Philosophy

Farm to Flame

Every morning, our team visits local farms within 100km of Hyderabad. We build real relationships with the people who grow our food — knowing them by name, understanding their seasons, and adjusting our menu to what's at its best.

This isn't a marketing slogan. It's the reason our food tastes different. Freshness you can taste in every bite.
